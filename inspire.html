What makes Lefty SM a hero?

Lefty was a Mexican rapper from the state of Sonora. During his career, he was able to pull in millions of streams and became one of Mexico's biggest artists.

Unfortunately, Lefty was murdered in September of 2023. His death was tragic and fans were mouring the loss of a great talent. Lefty is my hero because he means what he says. In a lot of his music, he describes himself as someone who is somewhat of a badass.

I never doubted it, but it was proven true when video footage of his murder was released. Even when he was outnumbered, three to one, he was able to put up a fight againts his murderers. I highly respect him for having grit and reminds me to strive forward no matter the odds.
